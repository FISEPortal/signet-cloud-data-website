Privacy-By-Design

Privacy is not a feature.
It is the foundation.

Privacy is a rare commodity in an increasingly digitized world. Signet was built differently. Privacy defaults are engineered into every layer of interaction, from registration and data upload through encryption, file sharing, and download.

Whether you are storing personal health records, neural data from BCI devices, financial documents, legal files, or original creative work, Signet applies the same principle: your data is private by design, not by policy promise.

No backdoors. No platform access. No third-party scanning. Only you hold the keys, and that is an architectural guarantee, not a setting you have to remember to turn on.

Data Access Control

Granular permissions.
Zero ambiguity.

Sensitive, high-value data demands more than a password. Signet embeds granular access control at the file level, ensuring that every piece of data you store or share carries its own permissions, expiration conditions, and audit trail.

You decide who gets access, under what conditions, for how long, and what they are permitted to do with it. That applies equally to a shared legal brief, a genomic dataset, a neural recording, or an original design file. The permission structure follows the data, not the platform.

When access expires or is revoked, it is revoked completely; not just at the interface level, but at the cryptographic layer. No access without explicit, current authorization.

IP Protection

Unscrapable. Unbreachable.
Unstealable.

Your original work, designs, ideas, authored content, research, source code, creative assets, represents value that existing cloud infrastructure was never built to protect. Signet changes that.

When you encrypt your intellectual property on Signet, only you hold the decryption keys. The file cannot be scanned, scraped, crawled, or read by any platform, AI training pipeline, or third party. It is mathematically inaccessible to anyone without explicit authorization from you.

Blockchain-anchored provenance creates a tamper-proof record of authorship and ownership from the moment of smart contracted NFT creation, giving your IP a verifiable chain of custody that holds up in legal and commercial contexts. First to NFT with smart contract protections establishes your claim.

Data Compliance

Built to the gold standard.
Engineered into every interaction.

Signet is designed around the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR); the most rigorous data privacy standard in existence, and extends its principles to cover the data types that existing compliance frameworks have not yet caught up with, including neural, biometric, and genomic data.

Data ownership, security, privacy, authorization, and permissions are not checkboxes on a compliance audit. They are engineered into every user interaction: storing, encrypting, sharing, and decrypting data.

As neurorights legislation expands across jurisdictions, Colorado, California, Montana, and Connecticut, with federal and international frameworks soon to follow, Signet provides the technical compliance infrastructure that keeps you ahead of the regulatory curve, not scrambling to catch up with it.
