Privacy By Design

Animated graphic conveying Privacy By Design — privacy built into the foundation of a system

Privacy is a rare luxury in an increasingly digitized world. To address the need for cloud data privacy, we built into the foundation privacy defaults for personally identifiable data at every level of interaction, from registration to data upload, encryption, file sharing, and downloading.
Data Access Control

Authorized user inside a protective circle sending keys to vaults, while attackers try and fail to breach the vaults directly

Ensuring your sensitive, high-value files are encrypted and secured with permissions are standard procedure with Signet. We embedded granular data access control as a core native-security prevention mechanism for Signet users when storing data in the cloud and file sharing.

IP Protection

Animated graphic showing intellectual property protected by encryption — unscrapable, unbreachable, unstealable, unscannable

Protecting your original designs, ideas, and authored content from digital threats such as data breaches, scraping, crawling, scanning, and stealing come standard on Signet. When you encrypt your intellectual property files on Signet, only you have the keys and authorization to unlock the files.
Data Compliance

Animated graphic conveying Data Compliance — GDPR standards covering security, permissions, authorized users, privacy, and ownership

We embrace the gold standard of data compliance enforced by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Thoughtfully designed, Signet facilitates data ownership, security, privacy, authorization, and permissions, engineered into the architecture of users’ data interactions in the cloud for storing, encrypting, sharing, and decrypting data.
