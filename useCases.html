Personal Use Cases

Your data. Your terms.

Privacy
Secure, Private Backups of Sensitive Files

Encrypt and store tax records, medical history, legal documents, passwords, and digital family heirlooms on private, permissioned IPFS nodes. No company can access them, and ownership is cryptographically proven via NFT. Built for anyone who doesn't trust centralized platforms with their most sensitive data.

Sharing
Privacy-First Family Sharing

Share vacation photos, home videos, or financial spreadsheets with family using time-limited decryption access. No permanent copy is kept unless explicitly permitted.

Estate Planning
Digital Inheritance & Dead Man's Switch

Store wills, funeral instructions, crypto seed phrases, or final messages. Smart contracts grant access to loved ones only after specified conditions are met, no legal intermediary required.

Monetization
Personal Content Creation & Monetization

Writers, photographers, musicians, and 3D artists upload original files. The NFT proves content ownership. Sell permissioned content directly to your audience.

Collaboration
Anonymous & Pseudonymous Collaboration

Researchers, students, and hobbyists share notes, datasets, or project files with collaborators while keeping authorship tied to a pseudonym instead of a real name. Built for privacy-conscious users and those operating in restrictive environments.

Business & Professional Use Cases

A data economy layer provisioning verifiable ownership, fair monetization, and granular permissions.

Healthcare
Healthcare & Medical Records

Patients hold the keys to their own encrypted health records, granting doctors, specialists, or insurers temporary decryption access only when needed. The NFT proves ownership. With no central database linking identity to records, a storage breach exposes encrypted files, not your health history.

IP & Creative
Intellectual Property Protection & Monetization

Law firms, inventors, and studios register patents, screenplays, music masters, or game assets. The NFT serves as a tamper-proof, timestamped proof of priority. Licensing or selling access to the files doesn't require giving up the underlying rights.

Real Estate
Real Estate, Title & Escrow

Store deeds, surveys, inspection reports, and closing documents, with buyers and sellers granted temporary access throughout the transaction. Each document carries a tamper-proof, timestamped record via NFT, streamlining document exchange and due diligence, including across borders, while keeping sensitive details private.

Research & Academia
Research Data & Academic Publishing

Scientists and universities store raw datasets, lab results, or papers, selling temporary access to other researchers or journals. Decentralized IDs let collaborators stay pseudonymous. Each file is content-addressed and tamper-evident, so any alteration is immediately detectable, supporting research integrity and reproducibility.

Journalism
Whistleblower & Investigative Journalism

Journalists and sources can upload evidence or leaked documents and prove possession via a tamper-proof, timestamped NFT record, while using decentralized IDs to keep their platform identity separate from their legal one. Temporary access can be granted to newsrooms or fact-checkers on a need-to-know basis.

Web3 & Development
Decentralized SaaS & dApp Backend

Web3 applications and startups can use Signet as the storage layer for user-generated content, from content marketplaces and private messaging platforms to stock media libraries. Developers can program paid access controls for their app, letting users unlock premium files or datasets temporarily or permanently.
